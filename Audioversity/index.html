Mission Statement

Audioversity is committed to providing the best up-to-date education to students in a field that is constantly evolving. We are dedicated to helping the next generation of artists express themselves through the medium of audio production.

Our primary objectives are to foster a ripe learning environment, and to help our students find and realize their artistic potential. To do this, we developed a vibrant community that consists of people of all backgrounds. We wholeheartedly believe music is meant to bring us together and that is our guiding philosophy.

Programs We Offer

Each program will be issued a certificate upon completion of the 45 credits required. Classes will be held at designated campus studios, always under instructor supervision. Average length of each program is 2 years.

Music Production and Engineering

The Music Production and Engineering program prepares students to enter the recording arts field while being up-to-date on the evolving tools and techniques. Students will participate in real studio sessions using Pro-Tools, and at the end of each semester record a live band. All of this taught through the instruction of seasoned professionals with real-world-experience.

Electronic Production and Design

The Electronic Production and Design program is for the students that are looking to learn soundscape design. Here, the focus will be using advanced plugins in Ableton to create film scores and video game projects. At the end of each semester, students will compose and produce their own electronic projects to be displayed with an accompanying media source.
